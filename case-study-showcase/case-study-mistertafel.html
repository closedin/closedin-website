01 The Challenge

Cracking an extremely niche ICP

Endresz is a Dutch women's fashion brand specializing in timeless, high-quality designs produced in small batches. They wanted to scale partnerships with independent designer boutiques across Netherlands.

The challenge: this ICP is extremely niche and hard to qualify. Many boutiques don't list themselves on LinkedIn or major databases, and not all shops fit Endresz's strict criteria (premium, multi-brand, high-end positioning). Expanding into multiple Netherlandsan regions required copywriting in local languages and different outreach strategies per market.

02 The Solution

Building the entire outbound engine from scratch

Jurre and the team at Closedin built the entire outbound engine from scratch. ICP analysis, lead sourcing, infrastructure setup, and a short personal messaging structure that works extremely well in retail outreach.

01
ICP Analysis & Store Targeting
Analyzed store types, revenue ranges, and buyer roles to define exactly which retail stores were the right fit. Mapped the purchasing decision process inside physical stores.
02
Lead Sourcing & Verification
Built accurate lead lists targeting store owners and purchasing managers directly. Only 900 individual contacts were needed to generate massive results.
03
Short & Personal Messaging
Used a short and personal messaging structure that works extremely well in retail. The first line explains why we're reaching out, the second offers a brief intro, and the final line contains a clear call to action.
04
Continuous Optimization
Optimized outbound continuously which improved results week after week. A/B tested subject lines, copy angles, and sending schedules using Instantly as the backbone for fast testing and safe scaling.

03 The Results

96 opportunities from 900 contacts in 30 days

In less than 30 days, Mistertafel went from zero outbound to 96 retail opportunities. From just 1,800 emails sent to 900 individual contacts, achieving a 35%+ reply rate and 10% opportunity rate. Proof that outbound works even if you've never done B2B, with the right systems.
